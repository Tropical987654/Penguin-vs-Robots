# 🚀 ULTRA DEFENSE PRO

A high-octane space arcade shooter built with HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript. Defend the galaxy against endless waves of enemies, collect upgrades, and survive as long as possible!

## 🎮 How to Play

### For PC Users:
* **Movement:** Use `W`, `A`, `S`, `D` keys or **Arrow Keys** to navigate your ship.
* **Attack:** Click the **Left Mouse Button (LMB)** to fire your cannons.
* **Upgrades:** When you destroy enough enemies, an upgrade menu will appear. Click on the desired power-up.

### For Mobile/Tablet Users:
* **Movement:** Use the **Virtual Joystick** on the left side of the screen.
* **Attack:** Tap or hold the neon **FIRE** button on the right side.
* **Navigation:** Touch-friendly UI for all menus and upgrades.

## 🛠 Features
* **Dynamic Difficulty:** Choose between 3 levels (Nebula, Void, Nova).
* **Level-up System:** Unlock Rapid Fire, Twin Cannons, or Speed Boosts during gameplay.
* **Responsive Design:** Works perfectly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
* **Neon Aesthetics:** Beautiful glow effects and smooth starfield animations.

## 🚀 Installation & Deployment
No installation required! The game runs directly in your browser.
To host your own version:
1. Fork this repository.
2. Enable **GitHub Pages** in the settings.
3. Your game is live!
